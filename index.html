The most beautiful locations

The sea garden

The sea garden is said to be the largest landscaped park in the Balkans.the garden gradually grew to reach 90,000 m2. Located along the city's coast on the Black Sea, the garden gradually grew to reach 90,000 m2.

The monument of Bulgarian-Solviet firendship

The monument of Bulgarian-Solviet firendship is 23m high at 95 meters above the sea. Under the visible structure there are bomb shelters. Over 10 000 tons of cement were used to get built. There are 302 steps leading to the entrance of the monument.

The lighthouse

The lighthouse is built in year 1983. It is called the red lighthouse because of the red light it emits.
